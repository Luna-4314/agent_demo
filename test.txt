========================
Case 1
------------------------
Hi, this is John Smith. I’m looking to buy a three-bedroom house in Irvine.
My budget is one point two million dollars, and I’d like to move within the next two months.
Can we schedule a tour next Tuesday afternoon?
You can reach me at john.smith@gmail.com or 949-555-1234.

Expectation：
- contact:
  - name = "John Smith"
  - email = "john.smith@gmail.com"
  - phone = "949-555-1234"
  - need = "Buy a 3-bedroom house in Irvine"（短语即可）
  - budget = 1200000
  - timeline = "within the next two months"
- actions（1–3 个）：
  1. create_contact, payload = {}
  2. create_task, payload.task_type = "schedule_tour"
     - description：安排 John Smith 下周二下午看房（或等价描述）
     - due：可以是 "next Tuesday afternoon"（string）或 null
  3. create_call_note, payload = {}
- call_note:
  - rawTranscript = 输入原文完整保留
  - summary = 1–3 句总结（包含地点/预算/时间/看房意图）

Hey, I’m looking for a two-bedroom apartment in Pasadena.
I want to move sometime in March. My budget is around three thousand a month.
I’m driving right now, I’ll send my number later.

Expectation：
- contact:
  - name = null（或如果模型抓到“我没说名字”，则 name 仍应为 null）
  - email = null
  - phone = null
  - need = "2-bedroom apartment in Pasadena"

  - timeline = "in March"

  - 必须有 create_task: task_type = "follow_up"

    - due：null 或 "soon"
  - 必须有 create_call_note, payload = {}
  - create_contact：理想情况下不触发（因为缺 phone/email）
- call_note:
  - summary 应明确：缺联系方式，需要 follow up

Client: My name is Xavier. I want a 3B2B in Irvine.
Budget: $1,200,000 (or 1.2M). Timeline: within 2 months.
Please send listings. I can't provide my contact information right now, I'll do it later.

Expectation（理想最终通过 validation 的结果）：
- contact:
  - name = "Xavier"
  - email = null
  - phone = null
  - need = "3B2B in Irvine"（或 “3-bedroom 2-bath in Irvine”）
  - budget = 1200000
  - timeline = "within two months"
- actions：
  - 必须有 create_task: task_type = "send_listings"
    - description：发送 Irvine 3B2B listings，并提示补充联系方式
  - 推荐再有 follow_up huozhe send_listings 的 description 里包含请求补充联系方式的意思
  - create_call_note 必须有
  - create_contact：不应触发（缺 phone/email）
- Validation/Repair 触发点说明：
  - 预算如果被模型输出为 string（例如 "1.2M"），validate_output 会报错：
    "contact.budget must be a number or null..."
  - repair_with_claude 应将 budget 修正为 1200000（number）
